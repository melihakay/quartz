$$
Y = B_{0} + B_{1}X_{1} + B_{2}X_{2} + \dots  + B_{n}X_{n} + \varepsilon
$$

Dimensional reduction:

- PCA
- Factor anaylsis
- Feature selection

- k-means

	- outputs binary

More over it deals with canonical analysis and panel analysis.

# Data Organization

Data is structured in the following form
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
v_{1,1} &v_{1,2} &v_{1,3} &\dots &v_{1,p} \\

\dots \\
v_{n,1} &v_{n,2} &v_{n,3} &\dots &v_{n,p} \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
Here we have $n$ observations and $p$ variables.

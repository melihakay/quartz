# Properties of distance

Let $d(x, y)$ be our distance function. Then,

1. $d( \cdot, \cdot) \geq 0$
2. $d(p,p) = 0$
3. $d(\theta, p) = d(p, \theta)$
4. $d(p, q) \leq d(p,r) + d(r, q)$

>[!info] Definition
>
Cofactor matrix $C$ of $A$ is obtained by calculating the minors for each entry in $A$.
>
>$$
>C_{ij} = (-1)^{i+j}M_{ij}
>$$
>where $M_{ij}$, is the [[Minor of a Matrix | minor]] of $A$.

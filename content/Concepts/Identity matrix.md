>[!info] Definition
>A matrix $I\in R^{n\times n}$ is said to be a identity matrix if it is in the form of
>$$
>I = \begin{bmatrix}
>1 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ 
>0 & 1 & \dots & 0  \\
>\vdots & \vdots &  \vdots & \vdots  \\
>0 & 0 & \dots & 1
>\end{bmatrix}_{n\times n}
>$$
>where $i_{ii}=1$ and $i_{ij}=0$ if $i\neq j$.

>[!info] Definition
>Let $z\in C$ st. $z\neq_{0}$. Then, 
>$$
\frac{1}{z} = \frac{\bar{z}}{|z|^2}
>$$

Remember the [[Modulus of a complex number | modulus]] is $\sqrt{ z\bar{z} }$. Then,

$$
\frac{1}{z} = \frac{\bar{z}}{|z|^2} = 
\frac{\bar{z}}{z\bar{z}}
$$
Also the division is possible
$$
\frac{z_{1}}{z_{2}}=
z_{1} \frac{1}{z_{2}} = \frac{z_{1}\bar{z_{2}}}{|z_{2}|^2}
$$

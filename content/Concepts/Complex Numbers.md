# Definition 

$C$, set of complex numbers is the set of numbers in the form of $a +bi$.

>[!info] Recall that $i^2=-1$

Therefore, 
$$
(a + bi)(c + di) = ac + adi + bci + bdi^2
$$
$$
(a + bi)(c + di) = (ac - bd) + (ad + bc)i
$$

but the summation is much more straight forward:
$$
(a + bi) + (c+di) = (a+c) + (b+d)i
$$

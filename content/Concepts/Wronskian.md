>[!info] Definition
>Wronskian of an ordered tuple of functions $f_{1}\dots f_{n}$ is defined as
>$$
> W(f_{1}\dots f_{n}) = 
> \begin{vmatrix}
> f_{1} & f_{2} & \dots & f_{n} \\
> f^({1)}_{1} & f^{(1)}_{2} & \dots & f^{(1)}_{n} \\
> \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\
> f^{(n-1)}_{1} & f^{(n-1)}_{2} & \dots & f^{(n-1)}_{n} \\
> \end{vmatrix}
>$$

>[!info] Definition
>Suppose that $f_{1},f_{2} \dots,f_{n}$ are functions of $t$. A function of the form $f = \sum_{i}c_{i}f_{i}$, $c_{i} \in R$ is called a **linear combination** of the functions. 
>
>If furthermore $\sum c_{i}=1$, then $f$ is called an **affine linear combination** of the given functions

>[!info] Definition
>Let $h(\theta)$ be a function of parameter $\theta$. It can be simply $h(\theta) = \theta$.
>Now assume that $\hat{\theta}$ is a estimator of $\theta$. Then,
>$$
>V(\hat{\theta}) \geq \frac{\frac{dh(\theta)}{d\theta}}{nI(\theta)}
>$$
>Observe that if $h(\theta) =\theta$ then derivative in the numerator is $0$ and
>$$
>V(\hat{\theta} )\geq \frac{1}{nI(\theta)}
>$$
>where $I(\theta)$ is [[Fisher information]].

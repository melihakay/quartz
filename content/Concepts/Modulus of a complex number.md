>[!info] Definition
>Modulus of a [[Complex Numbers|complex number]] $z$ is:
>$$
>|z| = \sqrt{ a^2 + b^2 }
>$$

Observe that:
$$
z\bar{z} = (a+bi)(a-bi) = a^2 - (bi)^2 = a^2 + b^2 = |z|^2
$$
Then, 
$$
|z| = \sqrt{ z\bar{z} }
$$
Only number  with zero modulus is $z=0$.

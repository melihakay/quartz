Procedure is as following

1. Obtain augmented matrix $\begin{bmatrix}A|b\end{bmatrix}$
2. Apply elementary row operations to the augmented matrix until you reach $\begin{bmatrix}E|d\end{bmatrix}$ where $E$ is in [[Echelon form|row echelon form]].
3. The columns of $E$ that do not contain a leading $1$ correspond to the free variables
4. The columns of $E$ that contain leading $1$'s should be expressed in the free variables.

>[!info] Definition
>Generalized inverse, or g-inverse, of a matrix $A$ is given by:
>$$
>AGA = A
>$$
>It is usually denoted as $A^{-}$. If $A$ is [[Invertibility of a matrix|invertible]], then $A^{-}$ is unique and equal to $A^{-1}$.

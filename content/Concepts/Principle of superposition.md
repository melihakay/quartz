>[!info] Definition
> Suppose that $f_{1},\dots f_{n}$ are solutions of first order [[Linear equations|linear equation]] $y'+p(t)y=q(t)$. Then any [[Linear combination|affine linear combination]] of these functions is also a solution. 
> 
> If furthermore $q(t)=0$, then any [[Linear combination|linear combination]] of these functions is a solution to the equation.
> 
> This theorem says that combination of solutions are again solutions, and is called **principle of superposition**.

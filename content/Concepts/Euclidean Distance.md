>[!info] Definition
>It is the geometric distance between two points
>$$
>d(p, q) = \sqrt{ \sum_{i=1}^k (p_{i} - q_{i})^2 }
>$$

>[!warning] Euclidean distance assumes same scale and variability on each axis.

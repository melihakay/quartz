>[!info] Definition
>An eigenvalue of a [[Matrix|matrix]] $A$ can be obtained by
>$$
>Ae = \lambda e
>$$
>where the $\lambda$ is the corresponding [[Eigenvalues|eigenvalue]] of the eigenvector.

One can interpret eigenvector as decomposing the matrix into independent pieces. See [[Eigendecomposition]].

>[!info] Definition
>A normalized eigenvalue $e_{n}$ can be obtained by
>$$
>\frac{e_{i}}{\lvert e_{i} \rvert }
>$$

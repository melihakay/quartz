An amazing team that I have been working with for 3 years. The team focuses solely in building a driverless electric racecar for [[Formula Student]] competitions.

![[mfr.jpeg]]

>[!tip] To learn more about the competition we are prepare for check [[Formula Student Driverless]].

As you can see from the photo no driver sits in the pilot seat. We aim to let the Wheelie, our mascot, drive the car. To do so we have been developing end-to-end autonomous racing stack so called [[Wheelie, An End-to-End Autonomous Racing Framework for Formula Student Driverless Competition]].

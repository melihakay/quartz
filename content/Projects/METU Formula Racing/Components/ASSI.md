>The definition of the AS statuses does not re- quire any information on the previous status the AS has shown. Therefore, the implemen- tation for determining the AS status can be done by transforming the flowchart given in the rules into a simple set of nested if-else statements that is called with its required in- puts during every software execution cycle. The computed result will then be passed to the ASSI, see chapter 6 and the data logger, see chapter 15. Thus [[ECU]] computes the [[ASSI]] status.
